HERIATGE LOCATION

GOLDEN TEMPLE DAMBULLA

Dambulla Cave Temple

Golden Temple Of Dambulla also know as the Dambulla cave Temple can be found in the central part of Sri Lanka located 92 mi east of Colombo and 45 mi north Kandy.This is the best largest as well as the best preserved cave temple complex found in Sri Lanka.More than 80 caves have been found here but major attractions are spread over five caves. Statues and paintings of Lord Buddha and his life can be found in these caves.

The Five Caves

Deveraja lena is the first cave out of the five.this is also know as “Cave of the Divine King”.At the entrance of the cave there can be found monastery recorded in a first century Brahmi inscription. You can also see a fourteen meter Buddha statue hewn out of the rock.Buddha favorite pupil Anandha can be seen at Buddha’s feet while Vishnu at his head.

Cave of the Divine King

The Temple is built by converting the five caves into shrine rooms. During Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa periods the caves were built at the base of a 150 meter high rock. These are by far the greatest cave temple found in Sri Lanka. Along the gentle slope of Dambulla Rock devotees and visitors can access the caves.The view of rock fortress Sigiriya is offered to us by the Dambulla rock as well as the surrounding flat lands.
